What we do

A considered approach to
risk, threat, and privacy.

Security that works begins with an accurate picture of the risk. Not an assumption, not a standard package, but a structured assessment of the specific threats, vulnerabilities, and circumstances that apply to the individual client. That is where every Fortis instruction begins.

Fortis Security Consult was established to deliver that standard of analysis. Our work combines security intelligence, physical assessment, and operational experience to build a complete and evidence-based view of the client's security position. Everything we recommend follows from that picture.

The founding team draws on backgrounds in UK policing, specialist close protection, and UK special forces - experience that informs both the methodology we apply and the judgement we bring to every client instruction.

Our position

We do not start at the property. We start with the client. Understanding the individual, their circumstances, and the realistic threat picture they face is the only basis on which genuinely useful security advice can be given. Everything else is guesswork dressed up as expertise.
